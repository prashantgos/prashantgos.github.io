About Me

Dr. Prashant Goswami is a Senior Associate Professor (Biträdande Professor) in the Faculty of Computing, Department of Computer Science (DIDA), at Blekinge Institute of Technology (BTH), Sweden. He joined BTH as an Assistant Professor (Universitetslektor) in November 2014 and was promoted after obtaining the Docent title in 2021.

He obtained his integrated B.Tech. and M.Tech. (dual degree) in Computer Science and Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Delhi in 2005, after studying there from 2000 to 2005. Following two years in industry, he began his Ph.D. studies at the University of Zurich in July 2007. He successfully defended his dissertation, "Level-of-detail and Parallel Solutions in Computer Graphics", in December 2011.

From March 2012 to April 2013, he was a Research Fellow at the gameLab, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, where he worked on geometric modeling. He subsequently joined the MAVERICK research group at INRIA Grenoble as a Postdoctoral Researcher (August 2013 – November 2014), focusing on real-time landscape-scale convective cloud simulation before moving to BTH.
